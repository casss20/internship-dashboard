## Application Strategy

### Resume Tips for Diversity Programs:
1. **Lead with NSBE/BSU membership** - put in header
2. **Languages section:** "English, French, Haitian Creole (fluent)"
3. **Research highlight:** "NIH-funded researcher" in summary
4. **Projects:** Emphasize AI/ML with social impact

### Essay Prompts (Common):
- "Tell us about a challenge you overcame" → Immigration/family story + academic journey
- "Why are you interested in tech?" → Health 4 Haitians automation story
- "How will you contribute to diversity?" → NSBE mentorship plans + multilingual outreach

### Timeline:
- **September-October:** Apply to Google STEP, Microsoft Explore
- **November-December:** Apply to Meta, Uber, Apple
- **January-March:** Apply to financial services (Goldman, Jane Street, Two Sigma)
- **Rolling:** Palantir, LinkedIn, Bank of America
